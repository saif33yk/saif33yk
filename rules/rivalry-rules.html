GENERAL RIVALRY RULES

Rivalry Declaration

  • Each gang can declare one rivalry per week
  • Declared rivalries last for 48 hours
  • A gang can only be targeted by one rivalry per week
  • No surrendering from rivalries - must face it for full duration
  • Only Senior Staff can suspend rivalries
  • Toxic behavior during rivalries will result in fines

Important: All existing server rules apply within the rivalry system. These rules ensure fair competition and prevent excessive targeting.

KIDNAPPING RULES

Kidnapping Regulations

  • Only allowed against rivalry gangs (no random kidnappings)
  • No third-party gangs can interfere in kidnapping situations
  • Backup support only allowed from your own gang with declared rivalry
  • Third-party members found in kidnapping situations will be banned
Ban Duration for Violations: 36 Hours (Interference: 4 Days)

ROB COMMAND RULES

Robbery Regulations

  • Must have gun pointed at player to use ROB command
  • Only allowed against rivalry gang members
  • Cannot rob Police, EMS, Weazel News or Government members
  • Cannot rob people in SafeZones
  • Allowed at spray zones against rivalries
  • Must have higher manpower to initiate robbery
  • Civilians cannot be robbed (including whitelisted job holders)
  • Cannot rob dead players

Note: ROB command availability is event-based and activated by staff at specific times.

Ban Duration for Violations: 36 Hours

VEHICLE & COMBAT RULES

Vehicle Pitting

  • Intentionally pitting vehicles or persons is prohibited
  • Exception: Allowed during declared rivalries
Admin Jail Duration: 4 Hours
