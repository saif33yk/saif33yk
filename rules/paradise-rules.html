GENERAL SERVER RULES

Event Disturbing

Intentionally disrupting official server events without proper RP reason. This includes interrupting event flow, killing event participants without cause, or otherwise sabotaging organized events.

Ban: 6 Hours | Compensation: $10,000

Cop Baiting

Purposely attracting police attention without legitimate RP reason. Examples include driving recklessly near police just to get chased, fake 911 calls, or intentionally breaking minor laws in front of officers to waste their time.

Ban: 12 Hours | Compensation: $15,000

Disrespecting Body

Unrealistic behavior around dead bodies including teabagging, excessive shooting of corpses, or other disrespectful actions that wouldn't occur in real life situations.

Ban: 16 Hours | Compensation: $17,000

Fail Roleplay

Engaging in low quality or unrealistic roleplay that breaks immersion. This includes meme characters, unrealistic actions, or refusing to participate in proper RP scenarios.

Ban: 24 Hours | Compensation: $25,000

Erotic Roleplay

Engaging in sexual roleplay or overly suggestive behavior. While mild flirting is allowed, explicit sexual content is strictly prohibited.

Ban: 24 Hours | Compensation: $25,000

Vehicle Death Match (VDM) & Intentional Pitting

Using vehicles as weapons without proper RP reason (VDM) or intentionally pitting/ramming other vehicles without roleplay justification. This excludes legitimate police pursuits with proper cause.

Ban: 24 Hours | Compensation: $25,000

Fail Initiation

Initiating criminal activities without clear and proper roleplay setup. All criminal actions must begin with clear verbal/written communication that gives the victim reasonable opportunity to respond.

Ban: 24 Hours | Compensation: $25,000

New Life Rule (NLR)

Violating the New Life Rule by remembering events from before your character's death or returning to the area of death within 30 minutes. After respawn, your character has no memory of events leading to their death.

Ban: 24 Hours | Compensation: $25,000

Exceeding Limit In A Robbery

Demanding more than allowed limits during robberies ($25,000 maximum from players). This ensures robberies remain balanced and don't unfairly impact victims.

Ban: 24 Hours | Compensation: $25,000

Safe Zone Violation

Committing crimes or initiating combat in designated safe zones (spawn areas, admin buildings, hospitals, etc.). These areas are meant to be free from criminal activity.

Ban: 24 Hours | Compensation: $25,000

Unrealistic Name

Using names that break immersion (e.g., "Xx_Sniper_xX", "Joe Biden", etc.). All character names must be realistic and fit the server's setting.

Ban: 24 Hours | Compensation: $25,000

Taunting

Excessive out-of-character taunting or harassment. While some IC trash talk is allowed, crossing into OOC harassment is prohibited.

Ban: 24 Hours | Compensation: $25,000

High Packet Loss

Consistently playing with high packet loss that negatively impacts other players' experience. Players must maintain stable connections.

Ban: 24 Hours | Compensation: $25,000

No POV

Failing to provide point-of-view (POV) footage when requested by staff for rule violation investigations.

Ban: 24 Hours | Compensation: $25,000
