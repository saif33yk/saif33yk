Essential Roleplay Guidelines

These rules form the foundation of quality roleplay in our community

GENERAL ROLEPLAY RULES

Event Disturbing

Intentionally disrupting or interfering with server events.

Example: Shooting during a peaceful car meet or interrupting RP events.
Ban Duration: 24 Hours

Cop Baiting

Provoking law enforcement without proper RP reason.

Example: Saying "thulla" to PD/EMS or intentionally taunting officers.
Ban Duration: 24 Hours

Disrespecting Body (18+)

Using inappropriate emotes or actions with bodies.

Example: Peeing/pooping on bodies or other 18+ emotes with corpses.
Ban Duration: 48 Hours

Fail Roleplay

Actions that break immersion or don't make logical sense.

Example: Jumping off buildings without reason, unrealistic actions.
Ban Duration: 24 Hours

Erotic Roleplay (18+)

Engaging in sexual or explicit roleplay.

Example: Using sexual emotes or text RP in public areas.
Ban Duration: 72 Hours

Vehicle Death (VDM)

Using vehicles as weapons without proper RP.

Example: Intentionally running over players without reason.
Ban Duration: 24 Hours

Intentional Pitting

Deliberately ramming vehicles to spin them out.

Example: Side-swiping cars during chases without RP reason.
Ban Duration: 24 Hours

Fail Initiation

Starting conflicts without proper warning or counting.

Example: Shooting without counting properly or giving warning.
Ban Duration: 24 Hours

LIFE & DEATH RULES

New Life Rule (NLR)

Cannot remember or return to previous life situations.

Example: Returning to a shootout location within 30 minutes of death.
Ban Duration: 24 Hours

No Value of Life (NVL)

Acting without regard for your character's survival.

Example: Jumping from moving vehicles or suicidal actions.
Ban Duration: 24 Hours

Revenge Roleplay

Seeking revenge for past situations your character shouldn't remember.

Example: Attacking someone because they robbed you in previous life.
Ban Duration: 24 Hours

ROBBERY & CRIME RULES

Exceeding Robbery Limits

Having more participants than allowed in robbery situations.

Example: Bringing 3 people to a 2-person limit robbery.
Ban Duration: 24 Hours

Safe Zone Violation

Committing crimes in protected areas.

Example: Pulling guns or kidnapping in hospital area.
Ban Duration: 24 Hours

CHARACTER & IDENTITY

Unrealistic Names

Using names that break immersion or are inappropriate.

Example: "Truck Wala", "Gunda No.1", or other non-RP names.
Ban Duration: 24 Hours + Name Change

TECHNICAL RULES

High Packet Loss

Playing with unstable connection during important situations.

Example: High ping/packet loss during Code Red or events.
Ban Duration: 24 Hours

No Point of View (POV)

Failing to provide recording when requested.

Example: Unable to provide footage when reported.
Ban Duration: 24 Hours

Meta Gaming

Using out-of-game information in RP.

Example: Checking player IDs or using Discord/Youtube info.
Ban Duration: 48 Hours

COMBAT RULES

Combat Reviving

Reviving or asking for revive during active combat.

Example: Getting revived while in shootout.
Ban Duration: 24 Hours

Combat Logging

Leaving game during active situations.

Example: Disconnecting during police chase or robbery.
Ban Duration: 24 Hours

Combat Storing

Storing items during active situations.

Example: Putting weapons in trunk during police chase.
Ban Duration: 24 Hours

Killing Out of Zone

Engaging in combat outside designated Code Red areas.

Example: Shooting someone far from active turf zone.
Ban Duration: 24 Hours

Random Deathmatch (RDM)

Killing without proper RP reason or initiation.

Example: Shooting random players without cause.
Ban Duration: 48 Hours

ROLEPLAY QUALITY

Low Effort Roleplay

Not putting proper effort into roleplay scenarios.

Example: Minimal responses, no character development.
Warning then 24 Hours

Power Gaming

Abusing game mechanics or position for unfair advantage.

Example: PD cuffing without reason, abusing animations.
Ban Duration: 24 Hours

Fear Roleplay

Not showing appropriate fear in dangerous situations.

Example: Talking back at gunpoint without fear.
Ban Duration: 24 Hours
