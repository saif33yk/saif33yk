General Robbery Rules

Fundamental rules applicable to all robbery scenarios

Mandatory Requirement

You must announce in OOC chat before initiating any robbery. Failure to follow this rule will lead to strict punishment.

Ban Duration: 24 Hours

Core Robbery Regulations

  • When 30 minutes of server restart notification pops up, you can't trigger any robbery (Ban Duration: 24 Hours)
  • Robbers must use masks during any robbery to avoid identification.
  • Tasing is prohibited during hostage situations unless the robber's gun isn't pointed at the hostage.
  • The hostage is the top priority in any situation.
  • Robbers cannot execute hostages without valid reason.
  • If caught, robbers must demonstrate Fear RP toward officers.
  • No Fail RP accusations without recorded evidence shown to staff.
  • You must have a hostage to negotiate with police or buy time. Without a hostage, police can initiate Code Red after 30 seconds warning.
  • Running during negotiations is prohibited - you must wait until negotiations conclude.
  • In hostage situations, robbers cannot make more than 2 (reasonable) demands.
  • Non-gang members cannot participate in major robberies (small banks and shops are permitted). (Ban Duration: 24 Hours)
  • If someone crashes/disconnects during a robbery, they cannot return to that situation. (Ban Duration: 48 Hours or 6H Jail)
  • Only pistols and pump shotguns allowed in shop robberies. Machine/AP pistols are prohibited!

Simultaneous Robbery Rule

When a Major Robbery is in progress, no other robberies can be triggered. You must wait until the current major robbery concludes.

Violation Punishment: 3 Hours Jail Time

Audio Restrictions

Rebels & LSPD are prohibited from playing loud music during major robberies.

Ban Duration: 48 Hours

Robbery Specific Rules

Regulations for each type of robbery scenario

ATM Robbery

  • 1 Rebel & 1 Cop maximum
  • Only Simple Pistol allowed
  • Direct Code Red prohibited - must RP first

Shop Robbery

  • 2 Officers & 2 Rebels maximum
  • Only Pistol & Pump Shotgun allowed
  • Machine/AP Pistols prohibited

Jewellery Store

  • 5 LSPD Officers maximum response

Fleeca Bank

  • 10 LSPD Officers maximum response

Main Bank Heist

  • 15 Rebels & PD maximum

FIB Robbery

  • 20 Rebels & 20 Police maximum
  • EMS revives prohibited
  • No carrying dead members
  • LSPD must respond via helicopter from rooftop
  • Gangs must enter from bottom floor only
  • No exiting until situation concludes
  • Must wait for robbery completion before Code Red

Humane Labs

  • Unlimited Rebels & Police
  • EMS revives prohibited
  • No carrying dead members
  • No exiting until situation concludes
  • Must wait for robbery completion before Code Red

Slaughter House

  • LSPD enters from both entrances
  • Rebels cannot fight outside before Code Red
  • Code Red initiates 60 sec after robbery completion
  • Outside fighting allowed after Code Red starts
  • No escaping via back entrance

Merryweather

  • Unlimited Rebels & Police
  • EMS revives prohibited
  • Code Red after 30 sec cooldown post-robbery
  • No rooftop helicopter landings (Rebels/PD)
  • Admin Jail: 4 Hours | Ban: 36 Hours

Chicken Labs

  • LSPD breaches from backside only
  • No flanking/escaping until situation ends
  • No interference from back entrance
