General Rules

  • Respect all players and staff members

Roleplay Guidelines

  • No random deathmatch (RDM)
  • No vehicle deathmatch (VDM)

Consequences

Breaking rules will result in:

  • First offense: Warning
  • Second offense: Temporary ban
  • Severe offenses: Permanent ban
