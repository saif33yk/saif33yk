PARADISE RP GENERAL RULES

1. Roleplay Standards

All players must maintain realistic roleplay at all times. No trolling, meme characters, or unrealistic behavior.

2. Character Consistency

Maintain the same character identity, backstory, and relationships throughout your playtime.

3. New Life Rule (NLR)

After death, you cannot remember events leading to your death or return to the area for 30 minutes.

4. Combat Logging

Disconnecting during active roleplay scenarios (especially combat) is strictly prohibited.

5. Event Disturbing

Intentionally disrupting official server events is not allowed without proper RP reason.

6. Cop Baiting

Purposely attracting police attention without legitimate RP reason is prohibited.

7. Disrespecting Body

Unrealistic behavior around dead bodies (teabagging, excessive shooting) is not allowed.

8. Fail Roleplay

All roleplay must be realistic and follow server standards. Low effort RP will be penalized.

MAJOR SERVER RULES

1. Random Death Match (RDM)

Killing players without proper RP reason or initiation is strictly prohibited.

2. Vehicle Death Match (VDM)

Using vehicles as weapons without proper RP reason is not allowed.

3. Fear RP

You must value your character's life in dangerous situations. No unrealistic heroics when outnumbered or at gunpoint.

4. Metagaming

Using out-of-character information in-character is strictly forbidden. This includes stream sniping.

5. Powergaming

You cannot force actions on other players (e.g., "I shoot you in the head"). All actions require consent.

6. Combat Storing

Storing vehicles or items during active combat to prevent them from being stolen is prohibited.

7. Killing Out of Zone

Engaging in combat or killing players outside designated PVP zones without proper RP is not allowed.

8. Permanent Ban Rules

  • Threatening other players or staff
  • Harassment of any kind
  • Scamming other players
  • Hacking or cheating
  • Bug abuse
  • Using third party software
  • Repeated abuse of rules

ROBBERY RULES

1. Value for Value

All robberies must provide RP value for both parties. No robbing just for items/money.

2. Minimum Participants

Store robberies require 3+ criminals. Bank robberies require 5+ with proper planning and RP.

3. Police Response

You must allow reasonable time for police response (minimum 5 minutes for stores, 10 for banks).

4. Hostage Rules

  • Hostages must be treated realistically
  • No killing hostages unless extreme circumstances
  • Hostages must be released when safe

5. Fail Initiation

All criminal initiations must be clear and unambiguous. Vague threats don't count as proper initiation.

6. Exceeding Limits

During robberies, you cannot demand more than the established limits (e.g., $25,000 maximum from players).

7. Safe Zone Violation

No robberies or criminal activity in designated safe zones (spawn, admin areas, etc.).

GANG TURF & TERRITORY RULES

1. Turf Claiming

Turfs can only be claimed by registered gangs with minimum 5 active members. Documentation required.

2. Turf Wars

  • Must be declared 24 hours in advance
  • Maximum 2 wars per week per gang
  • No attacking during cool-down periods
  • Proper RP must accompany all turf actions

3. Neutral Zones

Certain areas (hospital, police stations, admin buildings) are always neutral with no gang activity allowed.

4. Combat Reviving

Reviving fallen gang members during active combat is prohibited unless done by EMS with proper RP.

GANG RIVALRY RULES

1. Rivalry Declaration

Official rivalries must be registered with staff. Minimum 3-day notice before any hostile actions.

2. Hit Rules

  • Maximum 2 hits per day on rival gang members
  • No hits on unarmed or passive members
  • Must provide RP reason for each hit
  • No revenge killing outside of proper RP scenarios

3. Gang Alliances

Alliances must be roleplayed properly with documentation. No OOC alliances affecting IC decisions.

4. Taunting Rules

While some taunting is allowed for RP, excessive OOC taunting or harassment will be penalized.

FACTION RULES

1. LSPD Protocols

  • No corrupt cops without admin approval
  • Follow proper escalation procedures
  • Realistic response times and numbers
  • No baiting criminals into illegal actions

2. EMS Procedures

EMS must prioritize RP over quick revives. Proper medical RP required for all treatments.

3. Government Factions

All government roles require application and training. No abuse of authority.

4. Faction Requirements

  • Must maintain realistic RP at all times
  • Minimum activity requirements must be met
  • Proper uniform and vehicle usage
  • No mixing faction and criminal activities
