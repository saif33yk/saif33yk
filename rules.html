PARADISE RP GENERAL RULES

1. Roleplay Standards

All players must maintain realistic roleplay at all times. No trolling, meme characters, or unrealistic behavior.

2. Character Consistency

Maintain the same character identity, backstory, and relationships throughout your playtime.

3. New Life Rule (NLR)

After death, you cannot remember events leading to your death or return to the area for 30 minutes.

4. Combat Logging

Disconnecting during active roleplay scenarios (especially combat) is strictly prohibited.

MAJOR SERVER RULES

1. No RDM/VDM

Random Deathmatch (killing without RP reason) and Vehicle Deathmatch (using vehicles as weapons) are strictly prohibited.

2. Fear RP

You must value your character's life in dangerous situations. No unrealistic heroics when outnumbered or at gunpoint.

3. Metagaming

Using out-of-character information in-character is strictly forbidden. This includes stream sniping.

4. Powergaming

You cannot force actions on other players (e.g., "I shoot you in the head"). All actions require consent.

ROBBERY RULES

1. Value for Value

All robberies must provide RP value for both parties. No robbing just for items/money.

2. Minimum Participants

Store robberies require 3+ criminals. Bank robberies require 5+ with proper planning and RP.

3. Police Response

You must allow reasonable time for police response (minimum 5 minutes for stores, 10 for banks).

4. Hostage Rules

  • Hostages must be treated realistically
  • No killing hostages unless extreme circumstances
  • Hostages must be released when safe

GANG TURF & TERRITORY RULES

1. Turf Claiming

Turfs can only be claimed by registered gangs with minimum 5 active members. Documentation required.

2. Turf Wars

  • Must be declared 24 hours in advance
  • Maximum 2 wars per week per gang
  • No attacking during cool-down periods

3. Neutral Zones

Certain areas (hospital, police stations, admin buildings) are always neutral with no gang activity allowed.

GANG RIVALRY RULES

1. Rivalry Declaration

Official rivalries must be registered with staff. Minimum 3-day notice before any hostile actions.

2. Hit Rules

  • Maximum 2 hits per day on rival gang members
  • No hits on unarmed or passive members
  • Must provide RP reason for each hit

3. Gang Alliances

Alliances must be roleplayed properly with documentation. No OOC alliances affecting IC decisions.

FACTION RULES

1. LSPD Protocols

  • No corrupt cops without admin approval
  • Follow proper escalation procedures
  • Realistic response times and numbers

2. EMS Procedures

EMS must prioritize RP over quick revives. Proper medical RP required for all treatments.

3. Government Factions

All government roles require application and training. No abuse of authority.

COMPENSATIONS & BAN DURATIONS

Compensation Guidelines

  • Stolen vehicles: $15,000-$50,000 based on vehicle
  • Robbery losses: Up to $25,000 maximum claim
  • Improper arrests: $10,000 per hour detained
  • Character kills: Case-by-case evaluation
