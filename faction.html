Factions of Paradise

The law enforcement and essential services that keep the city running

Los Santos Police Department

Chiefs: Janjua & Zigana

The backbone of law enforcement in Paradise. LSPD operates under the motto "There is no faction above LSPD." Their minds are sharp and their tactics for controlling and eliminating crime are unmatched. With weekly strategy meetings and specialized units, they maintain order in a city full of chaos.

SWAT Team

Special Weapons and Tactics force with the best sharpshooters in the city. They're the elite Code Red response team that handles the most dangerous situations.

QRF

Quick Response Force specialists excel in negotiations and RP-based policing, providing rapid deployment to critical incidents.

PIO Unit

Public Information Officers handle joint operations and public affairs, ensuring smooth coordination between departments.

Elite Training Code Red Experts Strategic Operations City-Wide Presence

Federal Investigation Bureau

Director: Jani

The premier federal law enforcement agency with unparalleled crime control capabilities and undercover operations. Under Jani's professional and high-level strategic leadership, the FIB has become unbeatable in the city. Their operations are so covert that sometimes even other agencies don't know they're happening until it's over.

Undercover Division

Special agents who infiltrate criminal organizations, gathering intelligence and evidence without detection.

Tactical Operations

Highly trained teams that execute precision raids and high-risk arrests based on gathered intelligence.

Cyber Crimes

Experts in tracking digital footprints and uncovering criminal activities in the dark web and financial systems.

Strategic Genius Undercover Ops Federal Authority Unbeatable Record

Emergency Medical Services

EMS Chief: Bulla

The city's frontline medical responders, always ready to provide expert care in any situation. EMS operates around the clock with teams of expert surgeons, paramedics, and medical supervisors who handle everything from routine checkups to mass casualty incidents. Their rapid response has saved countless lives in Paradise.

Trauma Team

Specialized in emergency battlefield medicine and critical care, often working alongside LSPD in dangerous situations.

Surgical Unit

Expert surgeons who perform life-saving operations in the field and at the hospital with equal skill.

Medical Air Wing

Helicopter teams that provide rapid medical evacuation from inaccessible areas and major incident sites.

24/7 Availability Expert Surgeons Rapid Response Life-Saving Skills

Weazel News

Chairman: -

The eyes and ears of Paradise, bringing the truth to the public no matter how dangerous the story. Weazel News reporters risk their lives daily to cover breaking news, gang activities, and police operations. With camera crews embedded with law enforcement and contacts throughout the underworld, they're often the first on scene when news breaks.

Investigative Journalists

Dig deep into corruption stories and expose criminal activities through months of undercover work.

Field Reporters

Brave correspondents who report live from active crime scenes, gang territories, and disaster zones.

Production Team

Editors, camera operators, and technical staff who ensure the news reaches the public quickly and professionally.
